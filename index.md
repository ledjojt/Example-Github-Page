## Data Sources and Project Ideas


### 1. NBA Stats  (nba.com/stats)  
**Link:** https://www.nba.com/stats  
**Description:** The official NBA statistics platform. 
**Project Ideas:**  
- Model shot efficiency based on distance or defender proximity     
- Break down team shot profiles and spacing tendencies  

---

### 2. Basketball Reference  
**Link:** https://www.basketball-reference.com/  
**Description:** Comprehensive historical NBA statistics, box scores, advanced metrics, lineup combinations.  
**Project Ideas:**  
- Long-term trends in pace, 3-point volume, or efficiency  
- MVP prediction models using modern analytics   
- Modeling career aging curves for players  

---

### 3. Kaggle Basketball Datasets  
**Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?search=basketball  
**Description:** A large collection of user-uploaded basketball datasets, including NBA play-by-play, salary info, NCAA stats, shot charts, and prediction challenges.  
**Project Ideas:**  
- Salary vs. performance modeling  
- Identifying the most valuable statistical indicators for winning  


---

### 4. Synergy Basketball  
**Link:** https://apps.synergysports.com/

**Description:** Possession-level and play-type analytics, including pick-and-roll efficiency, isolation data, transition frequency. This requires a log-in that I have. 

**Project Ideas:**  
- Comparing play-type efficiency across teams   
- Identifying which actions generate the most points 

---

### 5. College Basketball (Sports-Reference CBB)  
**Link:** https://www.sports-reference.com/cbb/  
**Description:** NCAA basketball data including team stats, player performance, rankings, tournament history, and advanced metrics.  
**Project Ideas:**   
- Conference-by-conference efficiency comparisons  
- Predicting team success based on non-shooting metrics  
- Identifying the impact of player experience on wins
